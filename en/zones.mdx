Zones are shared workspaces where you collaborate with your team. A zone groups members and tasks together, so the right people can see and work on the same conversations.

## Create a zone

Select **Create zone**, give it a name and an optional description, and create it. The zone appears in your list with a count of its members and tasks.

## Invite members

Open a zone and select **Invite member**, then generate an invite link to share. Each member has a role:

| Role | Can do |
| --- | --- |
| Owner | Full control of the zone, members, and tasks |
| Editor | View and work on tasks in the zone |
| Viewer | View tasks in the zone |

## Add tasks to a zone

Open a zone, select **Add thread**, and choose the tasks to include. Members of the zone can then see and continue those tasks based on their role. Remove a task to take it out of the shared zone.

<Note>
  Deleting a zone removes the shared workspace. Confirm before deleting, since the action cannot be undone.
</Note>
