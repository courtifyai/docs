The knowledge base holds documents that Workers search and reference while working. Use it to make results match your standards, your templates, and the way your team reviews work.

<Frame caption="Knowledge base documents">
  <img src="/images/knowledge-base-en.png" alt="Knowledge base with uploaded documents" />
</Frame>

<Info>
  Enable the knowledge base in a Worker's configuration first. Until it is enabled, the Worker cannot search your documents.
</Info>

## Add documents

Select **Upload file** and choose the documents you want available. Each document can be given a role so the Worker knows how to use it:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Template" icon="file-lines">
    A reference document the Worker follows as a model, such as a standard contract form.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Experience source" icon="lightbulb">
    A document the system distills into review habits, so the Worker applies the way you check work.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

When you upload experience sources, the system distills review habits from them. This may take a moment while it processes your files.

## Organize and refine

- **Tags** — add tags to documents and filter by them to find content quickly.
- **Scenario** — set the scenario a document applies to.
- **Summary** — edit a document's summary to guide how the Worker uses it.

After you change a document's content or settings, the index rebuilds so search stays current. You will see a short "rebuilding index" status while this completes.

<Tip>
  Upload your strongest example contracts and playbooks as templates and experience sources. The more representative they are, the closer results will match your expectations.
</Tip>
