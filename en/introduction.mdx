**Legal Excellence, Starts Now.**

CourtifyAI is an AI workspace built for legal teams. You describe a task in plain language, and a Worker plans the steps, uses tools like web search and a virtual computer, drafts documents, and returns a result you can review and refine.

It is built for legal work specifically. Workers understand modes such as contract drafting, compliance review, intellectual property, litigation support, and distressed assets, and they can reference your own documents and templates through a knowledge base.

## What you can do

- Draft and review contracts, then compare versions and flag risky clauses.
- Run compliance and IP checks against your own playbooks.
- Search case law and public records through connected tools.
- Generate legal presentations, reports, and data analysis.
- Automate recurring work with scheduled triggers.
