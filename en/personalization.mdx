Personalization tells Workers who you are and how you like to work, so results are tailored across all your tasks without repeating yourself each time.

</Frame>

## Your profile

Set the details Workers should keep in mind:

- **Nickname** — how you want to be addressed, for example "Counsel Zhang".
- **Profession** — your role, for example "In-house Counsel" or "Contracts Attorney".
- **More about you** — context about your work, such as the types of matters and agreements you handle.
- **Custom instructions** — standing instructions you want applied to every task.

This information is used to tailor responses across all your tasks.

## Import memory from another AI

You can import conversations from another AI provider to auto-fill your profile, so you do not have to describe yourself from scratch.

## Other settings

From **Settings** you can also manage:

- **Theme** — switch between light and dark mode.
- **Account** — your sign-in details.
- **Knowledge base** — see [Knowledge base](/en/knowledge-base).
- **Billing and usage** — see [Credits and billing](/en/credits-billing).
- **API keys** — see [API keys](/en/api-keys).

<Tip>
  Keep your profile current. The more accurately it reflects your role and standards, the less context you need to add to each task.
</Tip>
