## Referral code

If someone invited you, select **Have a referral code?** and enter the code during sign-up. Both you and the referrer can earn credits.

## Reset your password

If you forget your password, choose **Reset password** on the sign-in screen and follow the link sent to your email.

<Tip>
  Once signed in, your workspace, tasks, and files stay available across devices. Use the same sign-in method each time to reach the same account.
</Tip>
