---
title: "Connectors"
description: "Securely store third-party credentials that Workers use while running tasks."
---

Connectors securely store credentials for third-party platforms. Once a credential is saved, it is automatically supplied to the Worker when it runs a task that needs that platform, so you do not have to paste keys into a task.

## Configure a connector

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open connectors">
    Go to the **Connector** section in your account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pick a platform">
    Choose a platform from the recommended list, for example CourtListener for US case law and court document search.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter the credential">
    Paste the API token or key for that platform and save it. The status changes to **Configured**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

You can update a saved credential at any time, or delete it to stop supplying it to Workers.

## Security

Credentials are encrypted at rest with AES-256-GCM, with keys protected by RSA public-key encryption, and protected by TLS in transit.

<Note>
  Only store credentials for platforms you want Workers to use on your behalf. Delete a connector when you no longer need it.
</Note>
