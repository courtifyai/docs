This guide takes you from signing in to your first completed task.

## Prerequisites

- A CourtifyAI account. You can create one with an email address during sign-in.
- A modern browser (Chrome, Edge, Safari, or Firefox).

## Run your first task

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open CourtifyAI">
    Go to [gaval.autopilot.law](https://gaval.autopilot.law) and sign in. If you do not have an account yet, see [Sign in](/en/signing-in) for every option.

    <Frame caption="Sign-in screen">
      <img src="/images/signing-in-en.png" alt="CourtifyAI sign-in screen" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Describe your task">
    In the task box, write what you want done in plain language. Be specific about the goal, the inputs, and the format you want back.

    > Review the attached SaaS subscription agreement and list any clauses that deviate from our standard playbook, with the risk level for each.

    <Frame caption="Describe the task you want your Worker to complete">
      <img src="/images/dashboard-en.png" alt="Task input box with an example prompt" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add context (optional)">
    Attach files or pick a mode such as **Contract Drafting** or **Compliance Review** so the Worker applies the right approach. You can also reference your [knowledge base](/en/knowledge-base).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Watch the Worker work">
    The Worker plans the task and runs each step. You can follow its progress, see the tools it uses, and read intermediate results as they appear.

    <Frame caption="A Worker running a task step by step">
      <img src="/images/tasks-en.png" alt="Task view showing a Worker's steps and outputs" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review and refine">
    Read the result, download any generated files from [Space](/en/space), and reply in the same task to ask for changes.
  </Step>
</Steps>
